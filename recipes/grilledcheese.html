Grilled Cheese

Description

A simple grilled cheese to eat on a cold morning

Ingredients

  • 1 block of cheese
  • 4 slices bread
  • butter
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Grate cheese
  2. butter both sides of bread
  3. add cheese on bread
  4. take the sandwich and toast on the pan until golden brown on both sides
  5. remove and consume
