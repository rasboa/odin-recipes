Chicken Akni

Description

Chicken Akni originated in India but has since become a popular Cape Malay dish. This curry and rice mixture offers a tasty family lunch and is quite simple to make

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces chicken (thighs and drumsticks)
  • 4 tablespoons masala spice
  • 3 cups basmati rice
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 6 medium-large potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • tuft of dhanya
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1 bay leaf, black cardommen and star aniseed

Instructions

  1. Fry the bay leaf, black cardommen and star aniseed to infuse the oil with rich flavour
  2. saute the onions
  3. add coriander powder
  4. add tumeric
  5. add chicken
  6. add potatoes
  7. add rice
  8. steam on low heat for 35 minutes
  9. consume
