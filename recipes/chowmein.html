Chicken Chowmein

Description

A lovely hot egg noodle dish, perfect for a cold day and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 cake egg noodle
  • 1 deboned chicken fillet
  • 100g cabbage chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • soy sauce
  • teriyaki sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • oil
  1. Cook egg noodles in pot of water until soft
  2. marinade chicken fillet with teriyaki sauce and oil
  3. fry marinaded fillet and add cooked egg noodle
  4. add conservative amount of oyster and soy sauce until salt preference is reached/li>
  5. dish and consume
